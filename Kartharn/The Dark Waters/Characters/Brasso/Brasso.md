## Backstory
- 25 years old
- Mother died in child birth 
- Father did not raise him for the first 14 years of his life.
- Raised by his orc master at arms, his nanny, and a scholar from Ebaria
- They imparted values on Brasso such as empathy for the poor (Nanny), honor (orc), and curiosity (Scholar). 
- The orc taught Brasso about old  orc culture including taking care of armor the language, and protecting the weak  
- His father is a brutal enforcer for leaders in the shadow lands
- When he found out how his son turned out he ordered that the nanny be executed. 
- Seeing this as a dishonorable act the order tried to stop it. Killing 5 of Brasso's fathers men before dying to Brasso’s father.
- Scholar only one who got out alive
- Brasso’s father then took over raising him
- Did so through cruelty and brutal beatings
- On a mission to Cartvaile Brasso met Johnathan, who say Brasso’s hatred for his father and the system he represented. 
- Jonathan referred Brasso to the order
- Brasso runs away at the age of 18 to join the order.**
