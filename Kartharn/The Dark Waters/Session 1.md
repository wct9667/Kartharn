## Encounters
### Combat - Roadside Bandit Attack
#### Overview
- **Enemies:**  5 Bandits
- **Environment:**  Road - with difficult rocky terrain and forest on either side. 
- **Tactics:**  Bandits are there for the loot, and will run when 2/5 of them are killed. They target the weaker members of the party first to attempt to intimidate the party.
- [[Ilmindel Aldarina]] is a 30 year old female human merchant. She has been ambushed by the bandits with her daughter while transporting cooking spices.

#### Overview
Players will explore town and talk to various NPC characters, learning about the town and its troubles. These interactions will be dependent on what the players decide to do in town.

Guards will appear to take the party to [[Calder Vane]]. If they refuse they will be taken with force, otherwise they will be escorted to the keep. If the players have done something to warrant arrest, this will be used as leverage.
