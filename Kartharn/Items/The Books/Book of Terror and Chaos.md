## Description
A vestige of an eldritch being, Â It offers absolution from the terror that the player(s) faces. When near the presence of the book, if it is unattuned, the player(s) receive a vision of this. They must succeed on a **Wisdom Intelligence or Constitution** saving throw of **17+** or they read the book.
## Abilities
Malignance: 1.

Once each day, The player must spread terror and chaos, if they do not, increase the books malignance by 1, and they receive a punishment. Otherwise subtract it by 1.

Players also get a bonus per day they choose to not perform the act. These stack with each other.
