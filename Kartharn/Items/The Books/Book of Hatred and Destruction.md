## Description
A vestige of an eldritch being, Â It takes the form of what the player hates the most, offering them the satisfaction of destroying what they hate. When near the presence of the book, if it is unattuned, the player(s) receive a vision of this. They must succeed on a **Wisdom Intelligence or Constitution** saving throw of **17+** or they read the book.

## Abilities
Starting Malignance: 1.

Once each day, the player must give into their hate, if they do not, increase the books malignance by 1, and they receive a punishment. Otherwise subtract it by 1. 

Players also get a bonus per day they choose to not perform the act. These stack with each other.

| Day | Required Act                                                                  | Punishment if Failed                             |
| --- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Destroy or damage something minor that you hate                               | Take 1d4 psychic damage                          |
| 2   | Destroy or damage something of moderate significance                          | Take 1d6 psychic damage                          |
| 3   | Destroy or damage something meaningful to you or someone else                 | Take 1d8 psychic damage                          |
| 4   | Destroy or sabotage multiple items or targets                                 | Take 2d6 psychic damage                          |

| 6   | Devastating act of destruction affecting others                               | Take 4d6 psychic damage + 2 levels of exhaustion |
| 7   | Perform an ultimate destructive act of hatred (permanent or narrative damage) | Take 6d6 psychic damage + 3 levels of exhaustion |

## Properties
Cannot be willingly unattuned to or left behind. The player that is cursed by this book must keep it in their possession. If the player doesn't hate something, they now have a hatred of something relevant per the DM's discretion.
