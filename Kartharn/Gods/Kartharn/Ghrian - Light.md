**Status:** Alive, Trapped  
**Description:** Second oldest god and leader of the pantheon, Ghrian was kind and spirited, but sometimes quick to anger. He is currently trapped by the [[Empire of Light]] and used as a magical battery.  

### Personality
- Very kind  
- Very arrogant  
- Always in good spirits, though sometimes quick to anger  

### Followers / Associated Organizations
- [[Empire of Light]]  

### Other Information

#### Current Situation
