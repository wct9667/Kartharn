**Status:** Alive  
**Description:** The oldest god, Mirin is a silent, observant force and acts as the jailer of the physical aspects of a defeated eldritch being, keeping them trapped across multiple dimensions.  

### Personality
- Did not speak often  
- Actions spoke louder than words  
- Good listener, open door policy  
- Non-judgmental  
- Enjoys a good joke  

### Followers / Associated Organizations
- [[Aroha - Mercy]] (residing with Mirin)  
- Secretive monks or cults devoted to death and balance

### Other Information

#### Current Situation
> Mirin guards the Threshold and the dimensions containing the aspects of the eldritch being.
